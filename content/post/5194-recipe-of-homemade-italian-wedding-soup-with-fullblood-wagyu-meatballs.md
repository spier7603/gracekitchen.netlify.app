PREPARING THE FULLBLOOD WAGYU MEATBALLS - Preheat your oven to 350째F.Combine the Fullblood Wagyu ground beef, egg, milk, minced parsley, minced oregano, grated parmesan, and panko bread crumbs into a mixing bowl.
